📌 Project Overview

An end-to-end HR Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI to analyze employee attrition and workforce trends.The dashboard helps identify key factors affecting employee turnover and provides actionable insights through interactive visualizations.

🎯 Objectives
• Analyze employee attrition patterns
• Identify departments and job roles with higher attrition
• Understand the impact of age, salary, education, and experience on attrition
• Build an interactive and visually appealing HR dashboard

📊 Key Metrics
• Total Employees
• Average Employee Age
• Attrition Count
• Attrition Rate (%)
• Average Years at Company

📈 Dashboard Insights
• Attrition by Gender
• Attrition by Education Field
• Attrition by Age Group
• Attrition by Salary Slab
• Attrition by Job Role
• Attrition by Years at Company

🛠️ Tools & Skills Used:
• Power BI
• DAX Measures
• Data Cleaning & Modeling
• Interactive Dashboard Design
• Data Visualization & Business Insights

📈 Key Findings:
• The company recorded an overall employee attrition rate of 16.12%.
• Employees in the 26–35 age category contributed the highest share of attrition.
• Higher employee exits were observed among Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives.
• Attrition was more common among employees within lower salary brackets.
• A large portion of employees left during their initial years at the company, highlighting early-stage retention challenges.
